Privacy Policy (Test Builds)

Effective date: 2025-08-29

This app does not collect, store, or share personal data. No analytics or crash reporting services are enabled in these MVP test builds. Data (such as settings or progress) is stored locally on your device only.

Diagnostics

Test builds may temporarily write minimal diagnostic logs on-device to help investigate bugs. These logs are not transmitted off the device by Braw.

Changes

This policy may be updated in future test versions. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

Questions? Email feedback@example.com.
